Creamy Garlic Butter Chicken

This Creamy Garlic Butter Chicken is rich, flavorful, and super easy to make in under 30 minutes! Perfect for a quick weeknight dinner, it features tender chicken breasts in a luscious garlic butter cream sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts (or 4 small, boneless & skinless)
  • 1 tsp salt & ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional, for color)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup heavy cream (or half & half)
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for spice)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Slice the chicken breasts in half if they are large.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Cook chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
  5. Remove chicken and set it aside.
  6. In the same pan, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  7. Add chicken broth, scraping up any bits from the pan.
  8. Stir in heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes.
  9. Return chicken to the pan and let simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
